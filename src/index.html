About Us

Scarabeo 7 is an offshore semi-submersible drilling rig for oil production. It is fitted with two heavy lift cranes that are capable of reaching all ends of the rig. It has good ship stability and seakeeping, better than drillships.

Services

Scarabeo 7 drills for so many oil companies including Shell, ExxonMobile, Total and other.

Main components of Scarabeo 7:

  • Motor or power source
  • Drill line
  • Derrick
  • Drill floor
  • Drill string
  • Drill bit
  • Casing head or Wellhead
  • Pipe deck
  • Mud tank
